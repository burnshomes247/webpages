How I Market
Your Home

Every listing is treated as a premium product. Here is exactly what I do to attract the right buyers, create competition, and get you the best possible outcome.

Before We Go Live
01

Pricing Strategy

We identify your home's price range together using comparable sales, market conditions, and property features. I monitor the market closely right up to launch day — because timing and positioning matter.

02

Pre-Market & Coming Soon

Before your home hits the MLS, I leverage my agent network and industry connections to build buyer demand in advance. By the time we go live, the right people already know your home is coming.

03

Professional Photography

95% of home searches start online. Your home needs to look exceptional. I work exclusively with professional photographers who know how to showcase a property at its best.

04

Professional Video

A professional walkthrough video gives buyers an immersive look at your home before they ever step inside. Video dramatically increases engagement and gives your listing a significant edge over static photo-only presentations. Recommended when it adds value to the overall marketing strategy.

Maximum Exposure
06

Zillow Showcase

Every one of my listings is enrolled in Zillow Showcase — a premium listing format that gives your home a unique map icon, prioritized placement in search results, and 75% more page views, saves, and shares on average.

05

Bright MLS & Portal Syndication

Your home is listed on Bright MLS with full exposure to every active agent and buyer in the region. It syndicates automatically to Zillow, Realtor.com, Homes.com, Redfin, and hundreds of partner sites.

07

Dedicated Property Website

Every listing gets its own professional, mobile-friendly website built specifically for your home. A clean, shareable URL you can send to anyone — no clutter, no distractions.

08

Agent Network Email Campaign

A targeted email goes out to local agents the moment your home is live. Agent-to-agent marketing drives a significant percentage of deals — especially in the first 72 hours.

09

Social Media Promotion

Your listing is promoted across social platforms to reach buyers who may not be actively searching but are passively open to the right opportunity.

10

RE/MAX Global Network

As a RE/MAX agent, your listing is exposed to one of the largest real estate networks in the world — including relocation buyers and international purchasers.

11

Open Houses & Direct Mail

Strategic open houses to capture active buyers in the area. Print and direct mail campaigns to reach neighbors and targeted prospects who may know the perfect buyer.

Throughout the Transaction
12

Personal Follow-Up on Every Inquiry

I follow up personally on every showing, every inquiry, every lead. Nothing falls through the cracks because there is no team member to drop the ball — it all goes through me.

13

Negotiation & Contract Management

When offers come in, I negotiate directly on your behalf — every call, every counteroffer, every decision. I manage the full transaction through to settlement so nothing surprises you at the closing table.

Condition and price determine 75% of your outcome. Everything else I do is designed to maximize the other 25%.

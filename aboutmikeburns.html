Personal

I have called the DMV home since my family relocated from Upstate New York when I was 12 years old. Growing up in Ocean City, I worked various jobs during my middle and high school years — from caddying at Ocean Pines Golf Course and delivering fudge to the Candy Kitchens, to serving breakfast at The General's Kitchen and cleaning glasses behind the bar at The Bonfire. One summer, I even delivered furniture at Resort Furnishings, where my mother was the manager — though three months of moving furniture was enough to convince me to pursue other career paths!

After graduating from the University of Maryland, I knew I wanted a career focused on helping people plan for their future. I began my professional journey at Transamerica Financial, providing financial services, then transitioned to First Atlantic Mortgage, where I gained valuable insight into the mortgage process. That experience became an essential foundation for my work with real estate clients, starting at Long & Foster and now with RE/MAX Allegiance.

I have lived in various parts of the DMV, including Rockville, Bethesda, Falls Church, Arlington, Dupont Circle, Logan Circle, Reston, and Herndon. This gives me a deep understanding of the region and the unique qualities of each area's real estate market.

I come from a large family with five siblings. I am married to my wonderful wife, Alicia, and together we have an amazing son named Mack.
